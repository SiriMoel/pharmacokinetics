An alchemy mod about managing your magical liquid intake.
This mod adds new materials, merchant npcs, growable plants with fruit, and a stat bar to manage.

Find new ways to ingest magical liquids, create new magical materials, and grow plants. 
Get addicted to magical liquids.
Be careful not to let the bar fill up completely...
